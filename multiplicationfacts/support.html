Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress by going to the Settings tab and tapping "Reset All User Data". This will clear all your practice history, achievements, and progress data.

What does it mean when a table is mastered?

A table is considered mastered when you've correctly answered at least 80% of the questions for that table. This helps track your progress and identify areas that need more practice.

How do I change the difficulty level?

You can adjust the difficulty level in the Practice Selection screen. Choose between Easy, Medium, and Hard to match your current skill level.

Can I practice specific multiplication tables?

Yes! In the Practice Selection screen, choose "Specific Tables" mode and select which tables you want to practice. You can select multiple tables at once.
